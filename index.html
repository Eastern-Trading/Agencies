About us

Eastern Trading Agencies is amongst the pioneers of distribution agencies in Faisalabad.

Established in 1978, under the leadership of Mr. M Rashid, we have come a long way from using hand carts and bicycles to having a fleet of latest vehicles for the execution of supplies for customers. Our relationship capital with the pharmaceutical retail, wholesale and hospitals of the city is extending beyond decades.
